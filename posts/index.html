Strategies to joining a new team

Thoughts and strategies on joining a new team Joining a new team is challenging. You’re the new person on the team, you’re unproven, you don’t know anyone, and you might not know what you’ll be working on. Overall, there’s just a lot of ambiguity. Having a base strategy and a couple clearly defined goals can help ensure the transition is a success. As a new team member, the primary goal should be acceptance within the team.
